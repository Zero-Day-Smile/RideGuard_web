# AI Models (Planned)

## Objectives
- Weekly premium estimation
- Income stability forecasting
- Fraud anomaly detection

## Baseline Models
- Regression for premium pricing
- Time-series forecasting for expected earnings stability
- Isolation Forest for anomaly detection

## Data Inputs
- Weather disruptions
- Traffic intensity proxies
- Delivery activity windows
- Weekly earning variability
- Policy and claim history

## Outputs
- risk_score
- recommended_weekly_premium
- anomaly_score
