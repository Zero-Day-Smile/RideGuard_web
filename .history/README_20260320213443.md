# RideGuard

Protecting every ride, every shift, every week.

RideGuard is an AI-powered parametric micro-insurance platform built for gig delivery partners. It protects weekly income when external disruptions reduce earning opportunities.

## Why RideGuard

Gig workers operate on weekly cash cycles, but traditional insurance is usually monthly or annual, claim-heavy, and poorly aligned with operational disruption risk.

RideGuard addresses non-damage income loss from:
- Extreme weather
- Platform outages
- Traffic shutdowns
- City-level mobility restrictions

## Core Product Pillars

- Weekly-first income protection plans
- AI-powered dynamic premium pricing
- Parametric trigger-based claim automation
- Instant payout simulation (UPI or wallet)
- Fraud-aware event and behavior validation

## Primary Persona

Food delivery partner (for example, Zomato/Swiggy rider) with:
- 6-day weekly work pattern
- Income tied to completed deliveries
- Low financial buffer
- High disruption sensitivity

## High-Level Workflow

1. Onboarding
- Connect delivery platform identity (mock)
- Grant location access
- Share weekly earning estimate

2. AI Risk Profiling
- Analyze weather, traffic, activity patterns, and income variability
- Produce risk score and suggested weekly premium

3. Policy Creation
- Rider selects weekly coverage (for example, INR 4,000 or INR 6,000)
- System quotes dynamic premium

4. Real-Time Monitoring
- Weather signal stream
- Traffic congestion stream
- Platform uptime checks
- Delivery activity telemetry

5. Auto Claim Trigger
- Example: rainfall threshold breach plus activity drop >= 40%
- Policy eligibility verified automatically

6. Instant Payout
- Trigger payout through mock UPI or wallet rails
- Target processing under 5 minutes

## Premium Logic (Conceptual)

Weekly Premium =
(Base Risk x Location Risk Weight x Weather Probability Factor x Income Variability Score)
- Reliability Discount

Premiums are recalculated weekly based on updated behavior and disruption data.

## Feature-to-Hackathon Mapping

### AI-Powered Risk Assessment
- Regression model for weekly premium pricing
- Time-series model for income stability forecasting
- Composite risk scoring engine

### Intelligent Fraud Detection
- Location spoof detection
- Duplicate claim checks
- Activity inconsistency detection
- Artificial inactivity identification

Recommended baseline methods:
- Isolation Forest
- Geo-validation
- Behavioral anomaly heuristics

### Parametric Automation
Trigger examples:
- Heavy rain: rainfall > threshold
- Platform downtime: API inactive > 30 minutes
- Traffic shutdown: congestion spike
- Mobility restriction: area activity collapse

### Integration Layer (Hackathon-Friendly)
- Weather API: OpenWeatherMap free tier
- Traffic API: mock congestion service
- Platform data: simulated activity API
- Payments: Razorpay sandbox or mock UPI
